ICTAK has been pioneering its training model over the years both for Online and Offline training. With over 25K students & 10K teachers and a similar number of executives being trained by us, we have created a unique hybrid model called Positive Engagement in Real-Time Learning (PERL) model that has produced over 4.5 positive feedback ratings for the training that were conducted by ICT Academy of Kerala amongst its learners..

Postgraduate Program

ICT Academy of Kerala offers specialized Postgraduate Programs (PGP) on advanced technology skills in a hybrid model of self-paced and instructor-led approach. The overall duration of the program will be one year, out of which eight months gets dedicated to classwork, two months for the capstone project, and two months for an internship. Our PGPs will be suited for graduates, postgraduates, and working professionals with the aim of skilling, upskilling and reskilling.

Long-term Training

ICTAK Academy of Kerala offers specialized Long-Term Training programmes on advanced technology skills in a hybrid model of self-paced and instructor-led approach. The overall duration of the programme will be of six months; where the first four months will be dedicated for the learning phase, fifth month will be directed with a project and last month will be enhanced with a hands-on internship through TCSiON. Our long-term training programme will be suited for graduates, post graduates and working professionals with the aim of skilling, upskilling and reskilling.

Microskills Programs
