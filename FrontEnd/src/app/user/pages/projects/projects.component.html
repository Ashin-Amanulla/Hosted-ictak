Projects

ICT Academy of Kerala is involved with a lot of projects aiming to allow the youth to upskill their talents. These projects involve both government and other private initiatives.

KKEM

The Kerala Knowledge Economy Mission (KKEM) is a digital platform-based strategic program launched by the Government of Kerala to transform the State into a knowledge economy. The impact of the global pandemic and the advances in technology are transforming people's lives across the world. The lifestyle, career, culture, and societal norms have changed and are likely to change further. One major shift, perhaps in the most unprecedented ways, is in job engagement and career aspects. It highlights the need to think beyond conventional modes of employment and employability enablement. Considering the scenario, the Government of Kerala has embarked on the Kerala Knowledge Economy Mission (KKEM), with a vision to develop Kerala into a Knowledge Society and thereby achieve economic growth and lifestyle improvements for its citizens.

Young Innovators Programme (YIP)

The Young Innovators Programme (YIP) is a flagship program of the Kerala Development & Innovation Strategic Council (K-DISC) to promote a culture of innovation among the youth of Kerala. YIP was launched on 24th March 2018 to empower future innovators to innovate new products, services or models to meet emerging requirements, unarticulated needs, or existing market needs of the society more effectively through specially designed challenges. The program is open to all students studying above class 9 across Kerala. This includes School, ITI & College students. The first edition of YIP started in 2018 and is reaching the closing stage. The next three editions, started in 2019, 2020 and 2021, are currently in progress.

Skill Delivery Platform Kerala (SDPK)

The Skill Delivery Platform Kerala (SDPK) is a unique initiative by the Dept. of Electronics & Information Technology, Government of Kerala implemented through the special purpose vehicle, KSITIL and ICT Academy of Kerala (ICTAK) as the operating partner. The vision of Skills Delivery Platform (SDPK) is to collaborate with Industry experts/content providers and facilitate delivery of industry relevant employability skills programmes to students (and Faculty Development Programme for teachers) of Engineering, Polytechnic and other graduations across the state.

The SDPK is conceptualized using State-of-the-art Technology (Cisco Telepresence Solution) enabled learning platform that will enable remote delivery of high-quality skills programmes delivered by the industry. SDPK will connect 150 Hi-Tech classrooms in the Engineering Colleges/Polytechnic Institutes/Community Skills Parks in the state and the Industry Hubs such as Technopark, Infopark and Cyberpark. The highlight of SDPK is partnering with industry to deliver world-class skill development training programmes which will help students from all branches of engineering to interact with industry experts and get trained.

NORKA Skill Training

Aimed at addressing the issue of youth unemployment in Kerala, NORKA Roots is providing an opportunity to study job-oriented new-generation courses with a scholarship. Norka Roots is offering these courses in association with the ICT Academy of Kerala, an initiative of the Government of India in collaboration with state governments and industries. Subjects included are Robotic Process Automation, Data Science and Analytics, Full Stack Development, Cyber Security Analyst, Software Testing, and Digital Marketing. All these courses have a great demand in the industry.

ABCD

Accelerated Blockchain Development (ABCD) Programme is a technology training programme of high industrial relevance. By going through the programme, one will be able to achieve multiple certifications that will help to apply for different job roles in many information technology organisations worldwide.
