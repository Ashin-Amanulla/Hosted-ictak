Corporate Functions

The Corporate Functions of the ICT Academy of Kerala include Administration, Finance, Human resources, Legal, and Internal IT. The unit plays a significant role in facilitating seamless integration between other operations units of ICTAK for maximum productivity.

Administration

Administration functions are handled by the admin team, who ensures smooth functioning of the workplace for all staff members to attain productive working hours for the organisation and keeps a good track of the office facilities at the Head Office and other regional offices at Koratty and Calicut. The team is in charge of providing all the required support to other operations units like, facility and logistics to conduct various training programs, accommodation arrangements, employee requirements, local purchases etc.

Finance

The Finance team manages the accounting and audit discipline at the Head Office and other regional offices following the standard accounting procedures. The team is responsible for the monthly financial reporting, budgeting, rolling forecast, cash flow management, A/R & A/P management, project-based account analysis, periodical audits etc. The team also ensures 100% compliance with GST and TDS procedures and filing. The unit strictly adheres to the financial policies and procedures to achieve the organisation's goals.

HR

The Human Resources function of the ICT Academy of Kerala is responsible for various associate-related activities of the company. It includes direct functions like recruitment, induction, employee engagement, payroll, HR legal, and exit management, as well as facilitation functions such as performance management, competency management, leave management, learning and development, etc.

Legal

ICT Academy of Kerala is a company incorporated under Section 25 of the Companies Act 1956, and as a 'public company' comes under the umbrella of the Companies Act 2013 and various compliance guidelines of the Ministry of Corporate Affairs. The Corporate Secretarial & Legal division ensures compliance with all the acts, rules, notifications, and guidelines issued by MCA and other central and state regulatory bodies and reports to the Board of Directors (BoD). The division is a vital link between the company and its Board of Directors, shareholders, government, and regulatory authorities. It is also responsible for facilitating and conducting board meetings, and general meetings of shareholders and the committees. The division acts as a single point of contact for directors and shareholders on corporate governance, secretarial and legal/compliance matters. It also represents the company before various authorities. It is also responsible for the efficient administration of a company and for ensuring that the decisions of the board of directors are implemented. Various business collaborations, joint ventures, and other associations (commercial/non-commercial) are facilitated and monitored by the department by firm documentation and execution of legal agreements.

Internal IT

The Internal IT division of the ICT Academy of Kerala is responsible for the technical management of the company. It includes networking, procurement of IT equipment, local server management, UPS, access and identity management, etc. of all the locations of ICTAK. It is also involved in managing the utilities such as phone, data, user accounts, email, internal portals, website, etc.
