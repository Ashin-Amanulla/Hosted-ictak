Corporate Operations

ICT Academy of Kerala envisions an empowered job force from the multifaceted skilling programmes we deliver. Being an important beneficiary and partner, corporate stakeholders have a key role from sponsorship, mentoring and providing opportunities, like internship and employment to the candidates. The Corporate Operating Unit (COU) of ICTAK manages the relationship with the partners and corporate stakeholders.

Partnership

The unit continuously indulges in establishing a healthy relationship with partners and stakeholders. This relationship has an immense impact on the opportunities to the end beneficiaries such as students, faculties and others. ICT Academy of Kerala has been a responsible intermediary through which the various CSR and other dedicated funds have attained better fruition through effective management and delivery. The team played a vital role in facilitating such responsibilities. The curated relationship dynamics with corporations has helped the identification, design and implementation of projects.

Delivery

The team undertakes skill development projects, corporate training for corporates and partners. The team is also capacitated to provide the manpower supply in specific areas.

Management

The unit facilitates the delivery of skill development programs through partners. The team owns, manages and executes various programmes in association with the Knowledge Office of ICT Academy of Kerala.
