Empanelled Trainers

Empanelled Trainers are the second arm of the ICT Academy of Kerala, which includes industry experts, freelancers, career break women, and faculty members passionate about training. These trainers who support various activities like instructor-led training (virtual and physical), project mentoring, bootcamps, and project evaluations are onboarded for one year on contract by ICTAK.
