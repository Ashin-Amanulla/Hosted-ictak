Government Operations

The Government Operations Unit (GOU) of ICT Academy of Kerala deals with government-initiated projects. With strong academic and industrial connections across the country, the team can serve on multiple fronts and has the flexibility to meet the changing needs in the public sector. The unit focuses on ICT capability building for different projects initiated by the government departments. The GOU, as a whole, works in a self-driven, dynamic, and goal-oriented manner focusing on socially relevant government projects.

Strategic Planning

The GOU is highly skilled in creating, implementing, and evaluating project strategies from scratch. The proficient team is equipped with the latest know-how and are capable of planning, forecasting, and executing projects in parallel. The team is also efficient in budgeting and allocating resources for several government projects that have a social impact on society. With minimum resources, we as a team thrive maximum output.

Methodical Execution

The unit adheres to the industry standards of providing the best quality services by comprehending the ground-level realities. With the project-oriented processes, implementing the technology in the best ways possible, the team has developed finesse over the years in value-driven projects. The well-documented and periodic reporting helps the team to make it better with every project.

Agile Management

Adapting quickly to sudden or emerging changes without deviating from the primary goal has become a vital aspect in the present times, which the team has fine-tuned lately. Reallocating the resources and using new technologies, the team makes it possible to deliver. Even during the pandemic, this capability came into play in managing various socially relevant projects.

Technology Implementation

GOU has the potential to redefine conventional processes using emerging technology in public sectors. The team also has proficiency in customizing and implementing based on the proposed requirements. The team is in turn capable of defining the processes in a technically-viable format to get the goals.

ICT Consultancy

The GOU can optimize the use of existing technology to develop and implement various project strategies. The team plays a very significant role in building the ICT capacity of the state by providing necessary solutions and techniques. The unit also prepares and delivers digital campaigning strategies to different projects offered by the public sector.

Media Production

We have a creative team responsible for creating and delivering several creatives, including multiple forms of graphics and video productions for different projects of the GOU and inhouse requirements. The team is also capable of preparing necessary textual content for the creatives as required. Apart from this, the team has the expertise to plan and conduct live events with customized streaming using broadcasting software and virtual meeting platforms.

Miscellaneous

Often clients may have other project-related requirements which may not essentially fall under the specific expertise of the unit. We are open to such added services and try to deliver them as a value addition to the project.
