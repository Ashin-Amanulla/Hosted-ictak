Build your Career with ICTAK

ICT Academy of Kerala (ICTAK) is a social enterprise officially launched on the 24th of June, 2014. The organization had a humble beginning providing skill training programs to selected academic institutions. Over the years, ICTAK has grown to a prime service provider of all ICT and innovation-related training and capacity-building programs in the state.

A New Beginning!

2022 January 03 marked a new beginning for ICT Academy of Kerala, relocating to the new workspace in the Thejaswini Building of Technopark Campus, Thiruvananthapuram. Dr. K.M. Abraham (Executive Vice Chairperson, K-DISC) was the Chief Guest. Mr. Tony Thomas (Chairman, ICTAK), Dr. Rajasree M.S. (Vice-Chancellor, KTU), and Dr. P.V. Unnikrishnan (Member Secretary, K-DISC) graced the occasion with their presence as distinguished guests.
